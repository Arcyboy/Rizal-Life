About Jose Rizal

Jose Rizal was a Filipino nationalist, novelist, poet, ophthalmologist, journalist, and revolutionary. He is widely considered one of the greatest heroes of the Philippines.

Works

Rizal's works include novels such as Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo, which are considered some of the most important works in Filipino literature.
